大类排行榜

本榜单数据来源于 PinchBench。本榜是「任务排行榜」之上的二次聚合——大类划分采用 PinchBench 官方分类，每个大类下的指标由属于该大类的任务横向聚合得到（成功率取均值、成本与耗时按任务数加和），并额外提供「总排行榜」覆盖全部任务。任务级指标的二元判定与计算规则详见任务排行榜页面。感谢 PinchBench 团队开放数据。
参考链接：PinchBench 任务详情 · PinchBench 数据来源
Data for this leaderboard is sourced from PinchBench. This view is a secondary aggregation on top of the Task Rankings — category boundaries follow PinchBench's official classification, and each category's metrics are aggregated across its constituent tasks (success rate averaged, cost and duration summed by task count); an additional "Overall" entry covers all tasks. Task-level binary validation and formulas are documented on the Task Rankings page. Thanks to the PinchBench team for releasing the data.
References: PinchBench Tasks · PinchBench API
